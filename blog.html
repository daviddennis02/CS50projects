My Journey to becoming a Web Developer

First, let me introduce myself. My name is David Dennis, and I’m a full-time front end developer with 6 months experience. In that time, I’ve worked as a front-end developer for both as an Intern and full-time position in a Fintech consulting firm.

This may sound like a short time. Yes I know. Within this period I have consistently been involved in building projects, work and collaborated with teams. So, it's ain't a short time to be experienced.


I have heard of stories where Developers with no Computer Science degree were doing excellently in their career. I wil like to say Computer science degree is NEVER a necessity to become a good Developer. Hey, It takes more than just a degree, you need to be hungry to solve problems through programming or development. The degree is good in hand and you can leverage on it if you have one. Also I have met Computer science graduate that have no interest in Software development. So it is more about what your keen interest is in. I was such a Computer Engineer graduate that fought with Software development for 4 years until I accepted to learn JavaScript. Now the rest is history. to be continued.
